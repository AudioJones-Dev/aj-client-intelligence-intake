# Florida Ramp & Lift Reference Implementation

## Purpose

Florida Ramp & Lift is the first reference client implementation for AJ Client Intelligence Intake.

This example should prove the intake system can support a real field-service business with:

- AI receptionist setup
- CRM context
- Contractor portal context
- Billing rules
- Manufacturer/product knowledge
- Field-service operations
- ResponseOS deployment

## Known Business Context

Florida Ramp & Lift is a South Florida mobility accessibility company.

Core services include:

- Wheelchair ramps
- Stair lifts
- Vertical platform lifts
- Vehicle lifts
- Service and repair

Known operational context:

- Works with residential customers
- Works with builders and contractors
- Works with WillScot
- Uses contractors / 1099 workflows
- Requires workers' compensation exemption handling for certain contractor workflows
- Needs a structured vault for AI receptionist and CRM knowledge

## Reference Use Cases

1. Generate dedicated Florida Ramp & Lift Obsidian vault.
2. Seed core business context.
3. Identify missing knowledge required for AI receptionist demo.
4. Build CRM context around customers, builders, and WillScot.
5. Model contractor portal onboarding and billing workflows.
6. Produce ResponseOS deployment brief.
