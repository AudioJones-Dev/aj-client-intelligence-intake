# Vision

## Purpose

AJ Client Intelligence Intake exists to create a repeatable system for converting client discovery into structured, validated, AI-usable business memory.

## Business Problem

Founder-led service businesses usually hold critical operational knowledge in scattered places:

- Owner memory
- Staff calls
- Text messages
- Email threads
- Field notes
- Website copy
- Pricing exceptions
- Customer relationships
- Vendor rules
- Billing habits

AI agents cannot operate reliably unless that knowledge is captured, structured, validated, and kept current.

## Product Vision

Build a reusable intake-to-knowledge pipeline that lets AJ Digital onboard any client into:

- ResponseOS AI receptionist
- CRM context
- Business memory vault
- Sales workflows
- Operations knowledge base
- Client-specific deployment brief

## Strategic Outcome

Every client onboarding should produce a deployable business intelligence package instead of loose notes.

## Design Principles

1. Capture truth before automation.
2. Separate facts from assumptions.
3. Mark confidence and source on every important claim.
4. Optimize knowledge for retrieval, not just human reading.
5. Make the process repeatable across industries.
6. Preserve human validation before production AI use.

## First Reference Client

Florida Ramp & Lift is the first reference implementation.

The Florida Ramp & Lift use case should prove the system can support:

- AI receptionist setup
- Contractor portal knowledge
- CRM memory
- Billing rules
- Field-service operations
- Manufacturer/product knowledge
- ResponseOS deployment
